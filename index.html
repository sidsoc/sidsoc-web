About Us

We at the Student Innovation and Development Society believe in giving students the ultimate advantage when it comes to securing that all-important much-coveted job! Our society was set up in 2014 in order to promote innovation and professional development by providing NCI students across all disciplines the opportunity to engage in projects outside of their academic studies. Our aim is that students build professional experience and develop their technical skills whilst operating in a real world environment.

What We Do

Workshops

We deliver workshops on anything from e-portfolio creation to special interests and skills gaps sessions.

Career Talks

We organise a range of talks delivered by guest speakers aimed at giving students a foot up in the jobs market upon graduation.

Team Projects

We organise a number of projects throughout the year that are designed to expose students to real-world project scenarios where business and technology meet.

Innovation

We constantly promote innovation via special projects aimed at bringing the very best ideas to life.

Contact Us

Please feel free to get in touch with us at any time to discuss current and upcoming events, projects or activities. Membership is open all year round so if you are interested in joining the Student Innovation and Development Society, get in touch with us and we will arrange it!
