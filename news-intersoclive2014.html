IntersocLive 2014/2015 Published on: 29/01/15

InterSoc Live took place on 16th January 2015 where a small number of NCI students across multiple disciplines collaborated to build a number of websites including: the Student Innovation and Development Society's website (www.ncisidsoc.com) and one for the Pokemon Society's upcoming Pokecon Ireland event (www.pokeconireland.com). Both websites were developed from concept to deployment within 12 hours.

On the day

The event kicked off at 10am and all team members worked tirelessly to deploy all three websites which were live by 8pm that evening. Junior members of the InterSoc Live team wrote website code which was then checked by the Software Developer and Project Manager and the websites were deployed at the end of the event.

Money raised for charity

This event was aimed at increasing students' web development and teamwork skills while at the same time raising much needed money for charity. SID Soc is delighted to announce that the first IntersocLive event raised which will be donated to SID Soc's charity of choice at the end of the academic year.
